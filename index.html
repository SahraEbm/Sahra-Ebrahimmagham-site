Overview

Sahra Ebrahimmagham is an electrical engineer focused on semiconductor device modeling and compact model development for advanced transistor technologies. Her work centers on wide-bandgap devices, especially GaN High Electron Mobility Transistors (HEMTs), and their implementation in circuit simulation environments.

She develops physics-based compact models using Verilog-A to bridge semiconductor device behavior and circuit-level design tools.
